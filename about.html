About Bloom Craft Gardens

For over 15 years, we've been transforming outdoor spaces across San Antonio with professional garden care, sustainable practices, and a genuine commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Bloom Craft Gardens was founded with a mission to bring beauty and balance to outdoor spaces. What started as a passion project in 2009 has grown into San Antonio's trusted name in garden maintenance and landscape services.

Our team combines practical gardening knowledge with creative landscape solutions to help clients maintain gardens they can enjoy year-round.

We focus on reliable service, attention to detail, and environmentally responsible gardening methods that benefit both your property and the planet.

Our Mission

To provide professional garden services that enhance outdoor living spaces while promoting healthy landscapes and environmental stewardship.

We Achieve This By:

  • ✓ Delivering consistent, high-quality service on every visit
  • ✓ Using eco-friendly products and sustainable practices
  • ✓ Listening to our clients and adapting to their needs
  • ✓ Investing in our team's training and development
